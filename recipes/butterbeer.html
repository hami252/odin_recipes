Butterbeer

Description

I, like so many others, am a huge Harry Potter fan. I have been hooked ever since I was handed my first book. The world is so wonderful and J.K. Rowlings was able to paint a vivid picture in my mind. As a kid I would wish I could visit Hogwarts and experience everything for myself.

Now that I am an adult I realized that my childhood dream of visiting Hogwarts will never come to fruitiion. So I must figure out the next best thing. And that brings us to this recipe. Today, I am sharing with you how to make delicous Butterbeer . This delicious drink truly brings out that butter-y flavor that makes it hit the spot!

Ingredients
  • 2 liters cream soda
  • 1/4 tsp caramel extract
  • 1/4 tsp butter extract
    • Cream Topping
    • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
    • 1/2 butterscotch topping
    • 1/4 cup powder sugar
    Instructions
    1. In large mixing bowl, whip heavy cream until it forms stiff peaks.
    2. Add butterscotch topping and powdered sugar.
    3. Mix the caramel and butter extracts with the cream soda and then pour the mixture into clear cups or mugs.
    4. Top with butterscotch cream topping and enjoy!
